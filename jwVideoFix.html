The Wave

The beautifully designed Wave is the ultimate hammock.

The 8.5’ long, 47’’ wide and 4’’ thick cushion provides ultimate comfort while the Batyline® fabric overhead provides filtered protection from the sun.

The Wave frame is made of stainless steel that has an electroplated finish which is highly resistant to corrosion.

Batyline is a woven polyester that is resistant to stretching, cracking and fading, very quick drying and resistant to staining.

The Wave base can be mounted in concrete or sand.

Furniture frames have a 10 year warranty against manufacturing workmanship and defects. Chair seat and back have a three year warranty.
